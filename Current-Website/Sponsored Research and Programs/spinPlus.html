Access Funding Opportunities: SPIN Plus site to connect researchers one to another and to find funding opportunities

To establish a SPIN Plus GENIUS University Profile follow these steps:

  • Go to infoedglobal.com
  • Click on the the GENIUS link along the top of the page.
  • Choose Create New profile
  • Select your institution
  • Fill out the New Profile Registration Page - select the department listed
  • Click Submit
  • You will now be at a page called profile summary page - do not fill anything out on this page. Scroll to the bottom and click Log Out
  • Your Administrator will now receive an email and they will need to validate your profile. Once validated you will then be able to sign into SPIN for added functionality and remote access.
