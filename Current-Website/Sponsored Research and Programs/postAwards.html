The Office of Sponsored Research and Programs Post Awards office offers assistance to FSU faculty, staff and administrators who have received funding from federal, state, or private agencies and organizations in support of promoting research and sponsored programs at FSU.  Throughout the life of the award your post awards administrator will work to provide advice and technical assistance regarding award management and compliance with sponsor guidelines. 

 
Please contact Carolyn Harris for assistance with non-financial award implementation and close-out issues.
