Our Purpose

The purpose of the Office of SPonsored Research and Programs is to ensure compliance with Federal and state laws and UNC policies; to clarify proposal submission procedures and processing of awards for external sponsored programs funds and to ensure a uniform administrative process among the constituent UNC institutions.
