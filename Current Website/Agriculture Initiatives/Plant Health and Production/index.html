Lieceng Zhu
Biological Science

Impact of environmental stress on the resistance of plant-parasite interaction

The goal of my research is to investigate, at molecular level, the role of environmental stress in responses of host plants to parasitic attacks. We use wheat-Hessian fly as a model system to determine the impacts of heat stress on metabolism of wheat plants infested by Hessian fly. The findings from our research will enhance our understanding of the mechanism of loss of resistance in wheat to Hessian fly and the vulnerability of plant resistance to parasite in the context of environmental stress and climate change.
